Highlights

Speciality Online Retailer

Overhaul of technology systems & processes transforms the business of an online gifting brand.

Retail, eCommerce, Order Processing, MoM and SoA

Order fulfilment on time is crucial for our client, who is into online gifting solutions characterized by seasonal demand spikes. As the systems are becoming unstable and unable to support the business growth and peak demands, Duvista stepped in with an integrated approach that focused on site stabilization and total systems re-architecture using emerging technology solutions. The result was a sound back-end with scalable & efficient order processing, supporting multiple sales channels and the resultant business growth. Solution Highlights:

e-Commerce provider of innovative reward solutions and gift products

  • Channels: B2B, B2C and Retail White-label
  • Users: 3 mil retail, 30K corporate, 50+ white-label
  • Geos: USA, Canada
KEY CHALLENGES
  • Order losses during peak traffic
  • Inefficient order processing
  • Offline steps in order processing
  • Application design constraints
  • Skilled team unavailability and ram-up
SERVICES

Technology consulting • Application Re-engineering • New architecture & features implementation • Database migration & optimization • Site stabilization and support • Test automation

TECHNOLOGIES
  1. Design: Message Oriented Middleware (MoM), Service Oriented Architecture (SOA)
  2. Programming: Message Queues, Java, J2EE, JMS, JBOSS
  3. DB, OS: MySQL, SQL Server, Windows, Linux
  4. UI: Web UI, ASP.NET
  5. Integration: Web Services, REST API, EDI, XML, JBOSS MQ, MSMQ
  6. Testing: ITKO LISA, QTP, LoadRunner
